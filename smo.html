Social Media Optimization (SMO)

Social media optimization (SMO) refers to the utilization of social media networks in order to grow a business's online presence and meet desired goals. This type of digital marketing strategy increases brand, product, and service awareness, helps build relationships with new and potential customers, increases traffic, and encourages sales (WebFX, Inc). According to research conducted by AdWeek, about 61% of consumers unfollow a brand because they deem the content no longer “relevant” and 20% will unfollow if a brand shares repetitive or boring content (Uifalean). This goes to show that there’s value in placing effort in understanding the relationship between content and the target audience. If you’re ready to optimize your social media profiles, here are a couple of ideas that will help improve your brand.

Keep It Personal

Keep your social media presence unique by giving it a touch of your personality. By using original ideas and creativity, your brand will stand out. Be innovative and come up with a voice that others can resonate with, this will make it easier to get followers. Try making your brand more personal by adding stories or anecdotes to your posts while keeping the tone natural, this will add value to the experience of the user (Blogger).

Tag The Right People

When publishing posts, tag individuals who you believe could increase user engagement. Don’t overdo this as it can become off-putting. Tagging can be done on platforms such as Twitter, Instagram, and Facebook, which will keep people engaged and talking about your brand. When you tag someone in a post, brand awareness is immediate (Blogger).

Quality Graphics

When it comes to first impressions, graphics are important because most people recognize the visuals before laying eyes on the content. According to HubSpot, whenever visual content is paired with written material, people usually retain a higher percent of the information (Uifalean). If you need help in creating quality graphics or editing photos, Canva is a great online tool to get you started. Instead of traditional text, try showing off your written content with infographics.

SMO is a valuable tool that’s useful to both individuals and businesses. By using these tips you will be on top of social media, user interaction will increase, and you will have a more reliable fan base. Your users will develop a strong following and will keep up to date with what you post, making it easier to reach your target audience and goals.
