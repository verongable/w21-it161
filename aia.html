AIA (Audience, Issues, and Approach)

Clients Name & Decision Maker Shane Gable
Website Name shanegable.com
Redesign or New Website? New Website
Type of Website Personal Branding/Social Networking
Client's Goals To make a memorable impact on potential employers in the tech industry by showcasing value and expertise as a software engineer and inspire those who are already part of or interested in becoming a back end developer.
Audience Issues, Concerns, and Needs Approach
Hiring Managers

Individual of a company seeking to fill an open position
  • In search of potential candidates that are fit for position at their company
  • Time constraints
  • If interested, they will need contact information
  • Provide information about Shane through 'About Me' section
  • Include all contact information
  • Highlight school and work experience
  • Provide easy access to Shane's resume
  • Provide easy access to social media accounts
  • Include intuitive userface for quick browsing
Experienced Developers

Individuals who have previous knowledge about software engineering. May also be an expert in other fields of technology.

  • Maybe interested in using Shane's website as reference for their personal branding
  • Used for source of inspiration
  • Looking to network
  • Interested in social media information
  • Provide information about Shane through 'About Me' section
  • Include all contact information
  • Include social media accounts
New Developers

Individuals looking to gain insight into the field. They may be younger than other audiences.

  • Less familiarity with terms used in software engineering
  • Less knowledge than experienced developers
  • Possibly looking to network
  • Also interested in social media accounts
  • All acronyms will be typed out
  • Include intuitive userface for easy browsing
  • Content will be divided into easy to understand sections
  • Include all contact information
  • Include social media accounts
